# Privacy Policy & Terms of Service

_Last updated: February 6, 2025_

Welcome to **Climb SNS**! Your privacy and security are important to us. This document outlines our policies regarding the collection, use, and protection of personal information when you use our services.

## 1. Introduction

By accessing and using **Climb SNS** (the "App"), you agree to comply with and be bound by these Terms of Service and Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with these terms, please do not use our services.

## 2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of data when you use our app:

- **Personal Information**:  
  Name, email, profile photo, and climbing activity logs when you sign up or log in via OAuth.
- **Usage Data**:  
  Information about how you use the app, including interactions with other users and posts.
- **Location Data (Optional)**:  
  If you choose to log climbs with location tagging, we may store location data.
- **Device Information**:  
  Type of device, operating system, and app version for debugging and improvement.

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

- Provide and maintain app functionality.
- Facilitate social interactions (e.g., showing your climbing logs and connecting you with friends).
- Improve user experience and develop new features.
- Ensure security and prevent fraud.
- Show personalized content, including advertisements.

**Note:** We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

## 4. How We Share Your Data

Your information may be shared in the following cases:

- **With Other Users**:  
  Your profile and climbing activity data may be visible to your friends or groups.
- **With Service Providers**:  
  We may use third-party services (such as Google Ads, Firebase, or analytics tools) to enhance our services.
- **Legal Compliance**:  
  If required by law, we may share your information with authorities.

## 5. User Rights

You have the right to:

- **Access & Update**:  
  View and edit your personal information at any time.
- **Delete Your Account**:  
  Request account deletion by contacting us.
- **Opt-Out**:  
  Manage your data sharing preferences in the settings.

## 6. Security Measures

We take data security seriously and implement:

- End-to-end encryption for messages.
- Secure storage for authentication credentials.
- Regular security audits to protect against unauthorized access.

_While we strive to ensure the security of your data, no online service is entirely immune to risks. We encourage you to use strong passwords and protect your login credentials._

## 7. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies and tracking technologies to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings if you prefer.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any significant changes will be communicated through in-app notifications or email.
